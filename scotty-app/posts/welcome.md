Welcome to the **Verdant Journal** — our little corner of the internet where we think out loud about gardens, landscapes, and the slow work of making spaces feel alive.

## Why a blog?

We've been designing gardens for some time already, and one thing we've noticed: the conversations that happen *before* a project begins are often the most interesting ones. What does this space need to feel like? What grows here naturally? What will this garden look like in ten years?

This blog is our attempt to bring those conversations into the open.

## What to expect

We'll be writing about:

- **Seasonal notes** — what's happening in our own garden month by month
- **Project stories** — the thinking behind some of our favourite commissions
- **Plant profiles** — deep dives into the plants we keep returning to
- **Design philosophy** — how we approach space, light, and living systems

We're glad you're here. Pull up a chair.
